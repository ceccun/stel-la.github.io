The Privacy Policy

Updated on Friday, 20 March at 12:00 ET

This Privacy Policy aims to help you understand clearly how your data and personal information is being handled across stella services.

When you start using a service.

When you use a stella service information on your visit is recorded for diagnostic and statistical purposes. Your visit will be counted for analytics on how many users use services. Your request will be handled by the nearest server in your country. Your IP address may be parsed by GeoBytes on some Bionic versions of our sites.

Advertising ID and Targeted Information

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to stella sites, services, products and/or other sites on the Internet. You have the ability to opt-out of personalized and targeted advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

Search on stella

When you search on stella, you will be interacting with the Google CSE platform to help you find results more easily. The search query will be handled by Blocks AI, a service by stella, to parse your search query and answer math and create paragraphs using extracts from web-results which will be displayed to you right after. If you are using the Bionic version and a compatible web-browser, your search query may also be processed by stella voice, a virtual assistant currently being developed by stella. The only pieces of information being collected here are:
•Your search query
•Advertiser Information

eMap (formerly stella maps)

stella collects information on how you use eMap and how it should be improved. Your cursor position, mouse-clicks, keystrokes and places you search for are logged. MapBox handles map content and data.

Speed

Use of stella speed is handled by Ookla.
