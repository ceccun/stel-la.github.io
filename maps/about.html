stella maps

stella maps is a mapping service to explore Earth in new ways. Use stella maps to find out where something is, or overview an area.

maps at work

When you connect to maps, you'll be greeted with a nicely designed map, a search bar, zoom and pan controls and the stella maps text. stella maps was built on-top of Adrenaline Engine 4 and is set to stay best optimized and fast.
