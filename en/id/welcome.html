Welcome to Stella ID

Your new Stella experience awaits, here are some highlights of what Stella ID brings to you.

Your Tabs, everywhere¹

When you use Tabs, you'll see Tabs that you've created in other places you're logged in, such as your phone or tablet.

Blocks Nano

Blocks powers search insights when searching on Stella.

Stella ID gives you access to Blocks Nano, an experimental and more helpful version of Blocks which is based on our language model.

Priority access

Stella ID members get access to helpful new features to test and shape the way Stella works for everyone else.²

¹Your Tabs are synced only when you opt-in to sync services on a per-device basis.

²Features are dependent on many factors and you may not receive every feature that is in the testing pool.
