Blocks Privacy

Stella offers an opt-in service called Blocks, which hosts either Blocks or Blocks Nano by Ceccun.

Blocks receives queries you make to Stella search and gives them additional processing using the Blocks model.

When Blocks receives your queries, it:

  1. Uses natural language recognition to convert your query into information a computer can understand.
  2. Uses that information to make additional search requests.
  3. Processes the information through a chosen model (Blocks Nano for Stella ID users)
  4. Returns the output back to you

Blocks reprocesses your queries into its models to improve itself and ensure that it can better process another query of the same time.

To verify its retraining, you may be asked questions on Stella search about your experience, which will display your search query as well as what Blocks interpreted of it.
