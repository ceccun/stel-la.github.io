Multilingualism

Stella aims to offer content and services to as many people as possible, including those who don't have the ability to read and understand English. Stella is always looking for new ways to translate services to other languages as well use translation data to improve machine learning algorithms in new ways.
This page aims to clearly structure and show you the open voluntary translator spaces that are currently open and the services they are for.
